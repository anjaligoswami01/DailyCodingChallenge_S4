# 📌 Day 4: Vectors — Teaching Meaning to Machines

## 🚀 Overview

Explored how **vector embeddings** help machines understand sentence meaning instead of just counting words.

---

## 🛠️ Tools

* Python
* Google Colab
* Sentence Transformers

---

## 📂 What I Did

* Generated embeddings for 5 sentences
* Compared them using cosine similarity
* Observed how similar meanings get higher scores

---

## 📊 Result

* Similar sentences → High similarity
* Unrelated sentences → Low similarity

---

## 💡 Key Learning

Embeddings capture **semantic meaning**, even when words are different.

---

## 🤔 Reflection

I was surprised that sentences with no common words still showed high similarity if their meanings were similar.
